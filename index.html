Who We Are

A studio
built on
craft

Founded in 2013, Tung Digital is an independent digital solutions studio working at the intersection of design, technology, and strategy. We are a small team of specialists who care deeply about the quality of our work.

We partner with ambitious founders, established businesses, and growth-stage companies who understand that digital excellence is not a cost — it's a competitive advantage.

Our Mission

To help businesses grow through thoughtful digital solutions that combine strategic clarity with exceptional craft. We measure success by the impact we create for our clients, not the awards on our shelf.

Our Approach

Every engagement begins with understanding — your business, your users, your goals. From there we move with precision, bringing strategy and execution together without the bloat of large agencies.

◈
Craft First
We hold ourselves to an uncompromisingly high standard — in design, code, and communication.
⬡
Honest Partnerships
We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Transparency builds trust.
◉
Outcomes Over Output

What We Offer

Digital services
built to perform

From initial concept to ongoing optimisation — we cover every dimension of your digital presence.

01
Web Design & Development
Core Service

We design and build websites that are as powerful as they are beautiful. Every project starts with a deep understanding of your audience, goals, and brand — then we translate that into a digital experience that converts visitors into customers. Performance, accessibility, and SEO are built in from day one, not bolted on at the end.

Custom design tailored to your brand
Mobile-first responsive development
Performance-optimised code
CMS integration (WordPress, Webflow)
E-commerce solutions
Ongoing support & maintenance
02
Brand Identity
Strategy & Design

A brand is more than a logo — it's the complete impression your business makes on the world. We develop comprehensive brand systems that define who you are, how you speak, and how you look across every surface. The result is a brand that feels consistent, confident, and distinctly yours.

Brand strategy & positioning
Logo & visual identity design
Typography & colour systems
Brand guidelines documentation
Tone of voice & messaging
Brand collateral design
03
Digital Marketing
Growth

Sustainable growth comes from a disciplined, data-led approach to marketing. We design and execute campaigns across paid search, paid social, email, and content — always optimising toward the metrics that matter for your business, not vanity numbers.

PPC & paid social management
Email marketing automation
Content strategy & creation
Conversion rate optimisation
Analytics & reporting dashboards
Campaign strategy & planning
