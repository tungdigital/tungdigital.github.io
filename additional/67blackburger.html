behind the scenes pix are FINALLY up!! November 14, 2003 — 11:47 PM
posted by: DarkDirectorMike | category: Production Updates

ok so i know i said these would be up like 3 weeks ago lol sorry about that. my geocities account got suspended AGAIN because apparently 50mb isnt enough for a horror movie website??? anyway i moved everything to this new host and it should be way faster now.

anyway THE PHOTOS. we wrapped principal photography on october 28th which was like perfect timing right before halloween. the grease pit scene came out INCREDIBLE. derek had to sit in actual vegetable oil for like 4 hours and he was NOT happy about it but it looks so good on film i dont even care. you can kind of see his face in photo #7 and its hilarious.

for people asking about the burger itself -- yes it is a real prop. our effects guy Ray built it over 3 weeks out of latex, resin, and some stuff he wont tell us about. it weighs like 14 pounds. it SMELLS. we asked him about it and he just said "trust the process." honestly at this point we do.

post-production starts January. we're aiming for a festival run in spring, then hopefully theatrical in the fall. keep checking back i'll try to update more than once a month this time lmao

-- Mike

filming has OFFICIALLY begun + production diary day 1 September 3, 2003 — 9:15 PM
posted by: DarkDirectorMike | category: Production Updates

WE ARE FILMING. I REPEAT. WE ARE FILMING.

after 8 months of pre-production, location scouting, casting, failed fundraising (thanks for NOTHING "uncle" dave), budget rewrites, a lead actor dropping out THREE DAYS before production (you know who you are), re-casting, more location scouting because the original diner burned down (unrelated to us), building the burger prop, and one incident with the health department that we are not allowed to discuss legally... we are ROLLING.

Day 1 we shot the opening sequence inside a real closed McDonald's in Barstow CA that a guy from Craigslist let us use for $200 and a case of pepsi. the fluorescent lighting in there is PERFECT. ray said and i quote: "this place already looks like a crime scene." he meant it as a compliment.

the burger showed up in a duffel bag. ray drove it from his apartment in Van Nuys. nobody asked questions.

full cast list posted on the cast page. check it out and leave some love in the guestbook for these guys, they're all working for nothing and they believe in this thing and that means everything.
